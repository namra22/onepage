ABOUT BAND

We love music


Karakoram is an alternative rock band from Lahore, Pakistan with a wide and unique soundscape from hard hitting rock to soulful spacey arrangements. The band includes Sherry Khattak on vocals and guitars, Omair Farooq on bass, Bilawal Lahooti on drums, Zain Peerzada and Annan Noukhez on guitars. Karakoram released their debut album titled 'Ailaan-e-Jang' in February 2021.
